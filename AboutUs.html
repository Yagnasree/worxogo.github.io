Our Story

Founded in 2014, Worxogo began with a simple question: could science-backed nudges unlock the next level of sales productivity? Partnering with Stanford’s Prof. Baba Shiv—the world authority on Neuroeconomics—our seven co-founders built an engine that blends cutting-edge behavior design with self-learning AI.

Today, the award-winning Worxogo Nudge Coach drives consistent, measurable productivity gains by adding a “behavior layer” to enterprise CRMs— no extra data entry required.
